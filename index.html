School Building

Spread in more than 850 bighas area, the school has extensive play grounds for Football, Hockey, Basketball, Hand Ball, Volleyball, Tennis, Cricket and exclusive grounds for parade and horse riding.

A thorough physical training is one of the chief aims. While a great stimulus has been given in this direction, teaching to help students excel in education is never compromised..

Ethics, Values and Student Life

The school strives to develop leaders who serve; intellectuals and scientists who innovate; artists who interpret, portray and celebrate life ; sportsmen who have the tenacity to claw their way to the fulfilment of their highest potential; competitors who develop the courage to embrace the glory of victory yet have the grace and fortitude to endure the sting of defeat!

Activities

For facilitating greater participation of students in co-curricular activities, in-house co-curricular activity (CCA) clubs are operational. All students are given the opportunity of participating in Inter-House sporting competitions in a wide variety of disciplines. Organized instructions and coaching in a large number of sports is conducted at the school in the evenings and is open to all. The school participates in district, state and national level competitions in a variety of sports.
