Transportation

The Transport facility currently includes 10 buses and 15 vans that are equipped with a Vehicle Tracking System (VTS) and on board cameras. This allows the School to monitor speed and location of the route. This effectively ensures the safety and security of scholars on board.
Students from around the city benefit from this facility, as the transport vehicles traverse through the entire region thrice a day.
